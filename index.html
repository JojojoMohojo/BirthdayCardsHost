THE RULES

You will have to draw a card if any of the following happens
  1. Tom tells you to draw a card, his word is final
  2. You place your drinks within the length of a thumb to the table
  3. You are caught drinking sparkling wine/prosecco (once per drink)
  4. You wish Tom a happy birthday
  5. Fail to split the G
  6. For each piss you do at the pub (first piss per pub is free)
You can make someone else draw a card if you do any of the following
  1. Wear the gamer vest for a whole pub
  2. You drink a glass of milk
You also have to draw one card for each hour you are late to arrive
